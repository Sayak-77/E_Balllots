About E-Ballots

Online voting is a unique concept on its own as voting is mainly conducted in physical mode , but along with that we have tried to many unique features to our website which not only help to make the website different from others but also provide various facilities to the user which can help them and also provide a great experience while using the website.

The user can create their own accounts, select their own passwords, vote according to their beliefs , read news and so much more, while helps to make the project as dynamic as possible and the project can have many new features added to the website which will help to make the website dynamic and flexible , helping the users to make their experience on the website more customized.

This site will help to make voting accessible to all, aged people who are not able to reach physical voting booths will be able to vote without any inconvenience, the project will help in ensuring that democracy is in right hands and the opinions of the people are voiced and not altered by intermediate people who wish to alter the voting process for their own benefits.

The risks associated with the project were low as our project didn’t require any heavy technology however there was a risk of database failure which was successfully prevented by the group members by making the link as secure as possible leading to better traffic management and better performance of the website as a whole.

It took 3.5 months to complete the project where around 5 days were required in order to gather the requirements, and another 5 to 6 days were required in order to analyze the requirements and develop a plan. Coding took the most amount of time as implementation of all the requirements was a tedious task, and the following span of 5 to 6 days will be given to each testing, deployment and maintainability respectively.
